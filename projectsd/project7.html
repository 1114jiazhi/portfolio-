A/B Testing for Revenue Growth - Sports Event Marketing Optimization

This project applies A/B Testing methodology and statistical inference to optimize sales strategies and marketing effectiveness for sports events, demonstrating my ability to translate data insights into real-world revenue growth.

Project Overview
  • Analyzed T-shirt sales data across multiple stadium locations.
  • Designed controlled A/B experiments to evaluate the effect of sales point proximity to concession stands on revenue.
  • Implemented post-game T-shirt giveaway campaigns and measured their causal impact on sales immediately after matches.
  • Directed and managed video production initiatives to increase fan engagement, leading to higher ticket and merchandise sales via social media channels.
Detailed A/B Testing Methodology
  • Randomized stadium locations into control and treatment groups for fair comparison.
  • Tracked key metrics: T-shirt units sold, revenue per visitor, conversion rates.
  • Applied two-proportion z-tests to evaluate statistical significance between groups.
  • Confirmed lift validity through p-value analysis (threshold 0.05) and confidence intervals (95%).
  • Controlled for time trends and external factors by using parallel monitoring groups.
Marketing Content Strategy
  • Produced short promotional videos capturing the excitement of live sports events.
  • Designed content optimized for social media platforms to drive viral sharing.
  • Observed a measurable increase in account followers and engagement rates after content launches.
  • Integrated ticket sales links and merchandise promotions into video campaigns.
Business Impact
  • Revenue from merchandise sales increased significantly post-experiment.
  • Positioning sales closer to high-traffic areas drove higher per-visitor sales rates.
  • Social media-driven marketing led to enhanced brand loyalty and increased ticket purchases.
Reflection

This project strengthened my ability to design and execute rigorous A/B tests, apply statistical analysis for causal inference, and integrate data-driven insights into business growth strategies. It demonstrates my readiness to apply both technical and marketing skills in dynamic, high-impact environments.
