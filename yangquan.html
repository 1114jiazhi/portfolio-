Yangquan, Shanxi - My Hometown

Where my story begins — a place that taught me humility, respect, and endless curiosity.

I was born and raised in Yangquan, a humble and beautiful corner of Shanxi, China. Growing up in a rural setting taught me some of the most important values that I carry with me today. From an early age, I learned the importance of respect — not just for people, but for the land, traditions, and community that nurtured me.

Life in Yangquan was simple, but it was rich in ways that go far beyond material things. Surrounded by nature and open spaces, I developed an early and enduring sense of curiosity. I spent countless hours exploring the fields, wandering through winding paths, and asking endless questions about how everything worked. That spirit of exploration, first sparked in my childhood, continues to drive my journey today.

Although I have lived in many vibrant cities around the world since then, Yangquan will always be the foundation of who I am — a place where humility, kindness, and endless curiosity first took root and grew.
